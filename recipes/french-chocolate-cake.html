French Chocolate Cake

This cake is not only simply delicious but simple to make! It is ultra dense and easily satisfies with its chocolately goodness!

Ingredients

  • 9 tablespoons 125 grams unsalted butter
  • 7 ounces 200 grams bittersweet 70/72% chocolate, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons water
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/3 cup 40 grams all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup about 30 grams dark cocoa powder, sifted if lumpy. Dutched or natural will work here
  • 4 large eggs, separated
  • 1 cup 200 grams granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • Two pinches sea salt

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Butter a 9-inch round cake pan. Line the bottom of the baking pan with a round of parchment paper.
  3. Melt butter in a large saucepan, 3 quarts is a good size. Once melted, remove from heat and stir in chocolate until melted. Once melted, stir in 2 tablespoons water, then baking powder, flour and cocoa until just combined.
  4. In a medium bowl, whisk egg yolks with sugar, 1 tablespoon water and vanilla, if using, until pale and no longer lumpy. Stir into chocolate mixture until just combined.
  5. In a large bowl, beat egg whites and salt until stiff. Gently fold into chocolate mixture until most of the white streaks disappear. Be careful not to deflated cake.
  6. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out batter-free. Remove from oven and rest on rack for 5 minutes.
